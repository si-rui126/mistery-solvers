Description

Gary “Game-day” Gristle is a middle-aged man of average height with curly dark-brown hair and a nicely trimmed mustache to match. He sports a college hoodie and sweatpants, or alternatively, jeans if he’s dressing to impress. When Gary isn’t working his odd jobs, he’s overseeing his daughter’s tennis practice. He’s also passionate about homebrewing, so he often makes own custom beer and kombucha at home.


History

All we know about Gary’s past is that he was a boy scout in his early years. His father, like himself, was a big sports fan. Later in life, he got married and had his daughter, Paige, but one day, his wife suddenly packed her bags and left, leaving him as a single father.


Personality

Gary is a friendly and down-to-earth guy upon first glance. However, he shows his questionable morals in certain situations, like when he told Scott to drive away and pretend like nothing happened when he scratched someone else’s car in a parking lot. He also doesn’t think there is an issue with him giving his daughter ability-enhancing brews before her games. However, he still cares for her because he is willing to give up the glory of winning for the sake of his daughter’s health. When she developed a dependency on the brews, he had to put his foot down and cut her off, as of now.
