Sleepers

They are “Sleepers”, “groggies”, “fogheads”; they are unable to see what you can see, no matter how concrete the facts you show them.

- City of Mist Player's Guide, p25

Sleepers have no resistance to the veil of the Mist. They are obsessed with their own lives; they don't care what's happening around them. The Mist makes it so that Sleepers just cannot see, not even what's right in front of them.

With her high pony-tail and runs as smooth as butter, Bariana Venti is no doubt a legendary pop star not only in the City of Mist, but world wide as well. Her concert tickets are extremely hard to come by, and you'd be lucky to even get your hands on an album before they sell out.

Eve is the daughter of Rachel Collins. She is polite to others and does not give in easily to peer pressure. Eve has a good relationship with her mother, as they enjoy spending time together, despite her getting embarassed a little bit sometimes. Like her mother, Eve is also a dedicated Christian. She is also fan of Amy's books, as she attended one of Amy's book signings. However, she only reads the ones with approved Christian values.

Paige is the daughter of Gary Gristle. She is a talented tennis athlete, making it to Regionals and States. On top of her talent, Gary supplements her training with his homebrewed performance-enchancing kombucha, infused with the power of the Philosopher's Stone. As a result, Paige is exceptionally skilled for her age, and has since developed a dependence on the kombucha. She believes that she cannot win without the drink, and she is addicted both to the brew and to the rush of winning.

Vivian is a party girl at heart. She loves her bleaching her hair, wearing false eyelashes, and going to the club. She has the men wrapped around her finger (including her ex-boyfriend Scott). She is attracted to rich, attractive guys, which makes it a complete mystery as to why she dated Scott. Vivian used to always use the people around her for money, but she tried to turn her life around by getting a job, which was working as a receptionist at Seven Strings Studios. However, she got laid off after the events of Next Top Sacrifice, so she is back to using men for their money. She is currently dating Daniel Pham.
