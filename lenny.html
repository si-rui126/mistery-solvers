Description

Lenny is a tall dark-haired man who dons a green suit and a top hat. It's obvious that he loves flexing his money, because he wears multiple luxury watches and loudly drives a garish green Ferrari.


History

Lenny used to be very poor until one day, he decided to follow a rainbow to see what was at the end of it. To his surprise, he found a man holding a small black cauldron. The man gave the black pot to Lenny, who then gained the powers of the Leprachuan.


Personality

Lenny is usually soft-spoken, but he loves bragging his wealth. He is also rather cowardly, as his creditor Fortuna scoffed at the idea that Lenny was capable of committing murder. He is also disloyal, since he was seen with many different women during his time at the casino. However, his days of playing around might be over due to Amy's asseration over him.

Abilities

Imperfect Counterfeit

The main source of Lenny's wealth is his ability to produce realistic and tangible bills from thin air. They remain just long enough for Lenny to make his escape, and then the illusion fades, revealing that there was nothing there at all.
