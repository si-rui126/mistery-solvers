Once upon a time, there was a mystical library hidden from the world of humans. In this library lived a young and handsome prince of the imps. He had paper white hair and a jawline sharp enough to give papercuts. Two long horns extended from the top of his head, and he was a fearsome sight to behold. Lounging on his throne of books, the imp looked up to the ceiling. Each day felt the same, and there was not a single living being in this realm other than the paper imps. The prince sighed… if only something interesting would happen. Suddenly, the ceiling started to shimmer and looked as if it had turned into liquid. A young woman with beautiful [H/C] locks fell from above. She landed on a library table with a loud thud, and let out a small yelp. “Oww… that hurt. Where am I?”

The woman looked around, and her eyes landed on the handsome imp in front of her. “Oh hello there, do you know how to get out of here?” she asked in a cautious voice. The imp rose from his seat, and made his way towards the woman. In a noble voice, he said, “Of course I know, but why should I tell you?” Upon closer look this imp prince was devilishly handsome.
