Description

Samuel is a tall young man with brown hair that he combs back into a sleek style. He sometimes wears glasses over his green eyes. As a hot-shot lawyer, Samuel’s attire usually consists of suits and ties. When he isn’t serving justice in court, he can be found walking his dog Barkiana or listening to music at home.

Personality

Samuel is very confident and assertive, as expected of a lawyer. There is nothing more important to him than punishing evildoers, and he will enforce it himself if he has to. He is also quick to judge people, as he didn’t hesitate to pull a blade on a teenager whom he thought was committing evil deeds. Additionally, Samuel is ambitious and good with his words, as he is able to leverage his assets in order for his boss to give him a promotion.
