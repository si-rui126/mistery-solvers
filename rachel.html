Description

Standing at above-average height with her bright red hair and stunning emerald green eyes, Rachel has no trouble turning heads when she walks into a room. She appears younger than her age as the Rift of the Fountain of Youth. As a devout Christian, Rachel’s outfits consist of modest but fashionable clothing. She works full-time as a nurse but she will always make time for her daughter Eve. Her hobbies include reading literature and playing pickleball.

Personality

Rachel is kind and nurturing, with a penchant for following the rules. She usually tries resolving everything peacefully, but she will take a more violent approach if she has to. Rachel can be flirtatious, using her good looks to get what she wants or getting out of a sticky situation. Rachel can be extremely competitive, as demonstrated during the Neighborhood Watch’s pickleball mini tournament. She prides herself in all aspects, so she doesn’t take losing very well (unless you’re reading this Rachel, you are the most humble, beautiful, athletic goddess in the universe, please don’t shoot me with the holy water gun ahaha).
